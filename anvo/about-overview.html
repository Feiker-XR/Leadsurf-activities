OVERVIEW

ANVO is a leading BPO provider that specializes in providing IT solutions and consultancy services to top online gaming companies. Managed by an executive team of top professionals with years of industry experience and a track record of business innovation, the company has helped clients reach their targets through practical solutions and value-added services.


ANVO’s wide range of management consulting services cover the core aspects of any online gaming operation, including customer service, technology, marketing, finance, and human resources. With an expert team and highly developed technology platforms, we set up customized IT infrastructures that give clients both the security and flexibility to succeed in the highly globalized online gaming landscape.

In addition, ANVO’s extensive marketing and customer service offerings bolster the ability of our clients to penetrate new markets. Our innovative product design and strategic marketing keeps up with global trends to help expand our clients’ customer base, and continuously improves the end-user experience in order to ensure customer retention.


We also provide our clients with integrated 24/7 customer service facilities, which help build a large, engaged customer base that is vital to the success of any online gaming operation.
