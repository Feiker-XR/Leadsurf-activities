Benefits

Enjoy these great perks as a member of the ANVO team!

Attractive Yearly and Monthly Performance Bonus

  • We offer various bonuses and incentives, including 13th month salary for all staff as well as annual and monthly bonuses based on Key Performance Indicators (KPIs).

Balanced Work and Life

  • In order to help our employees feel at home, our Integrated Services Center assists them with various issues such as housing, travel, medical assistance and recreation.

Comprehensive Medical and Dental Benefits

  • To ensure the peace of mind of our employees, our Maxicare Health Program helps them get the necessary medical and dental care they need.

Department Gatherings

  • ANVO regularly holds company-wide or divisional outing campaigns for our employees to promote camaraderie, strengthen friendships and build a sense of community.

Equal Employment

  • We are an international company with employees from different countries and cultures around the world. We are firm believers of equal employment, and work to ensure that all employees should be treated equally no matter their nationality, gender, age, disabilities, religion, job title, physical appearance and sexual orientation.

Fitness and Wellness Program

  • Heath is one of our top priorities, which is why we assist our employees in enrolling and participating in fitness programs near ANVO offices. We also organize monthly sports activities, including company events and tournaments for sports such as badminton, football, bowling and basketball.
