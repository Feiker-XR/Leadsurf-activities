Finance

We offer over a decade of finance and accounting outsourcing experience and a staff of seasoned accounting specialists and CPAs. Our experts will analyze client’s existing workflows and design an outsourcing solution that fits our client’s business objectives. Our full spectrum of Finance and Accounting Solutions ranging from transactional to complex services includes:
ACCOUNTS PAYABLE & RECEIVABLE:
Managing a small business involves many roles and responsibilities. In today's rapid business environment, running a small business demands quick and efficient business handling skills and strong financial management solutions to sustain profit. Sometimes small business owners are forced to handle several things at once to achieve their business targets.
TIME SHEET & EXPENSES:
Implementing Time Sheet application will deliver an immediate cost savings by eliminating several manual steps in our payroll process, thereby reducing our client’s labor expenses for each payroll run. By greatly reducing the amount of time of our clients and internal employees spend on data entry, we will provide a tangible return on investment and enhance employee satisfaction.
INVENTORY ACCOUNTING:
Our trained professionals can assist our clients on all stocktaking requirements. Customized stationery and floor plans can be produced and on-site supervision provided where necessary.
MANAGEMENT ACCOUNTING:
Accounting and financial reporting in accordance with international standards.
FINANCIAL ANALYSIS:
Financial statement analysis can be approached systematically by using a standard set of steps. This process helps reduce complex financial information into a common representative format that allows assessment of individual companies and comparisons between companies. Financial statement analysis also equips the analyst to calculate financial ratios that simplify the task of comparing results over time & to other companies
