Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

ANVO believes in a company's accountability to community. Hence, we take into account the social, ethical, and environmental effects of our activities with our staff and the community around us.


Corporate citizenship is one of our values that we strive to uphold every day.


With ANVO, responsible leadership is grounded in a strong ownership mentality, good governance, and accountability for our actions, financial discipline, and fair disclosure.


We build lasting and high impact relationships with the people we serve and the communities we touch.


ANVO is committed to improving and uplifting the lives of the underprivileged members of our society where our employees live and work. Corporate citizenship is one of our core values that are proudly embraced by our employees.

ANVO SHARES as it CARES.

We have been recognized for our strong commitment to social responsibility.


Our partnerships and alliances with the most accomplished, trusted, and credible non – government and government organizations on both the local and international level helps ANVO realize its drive to provide help to those who need it most – our way of giving back to society.

Community

With ANVO, we perform several community service initiatives aligned with our goals of:

  • Uplifting the lives of the underprivileged members of the society ;

  • Undertaking health, nutrition and medical projects to promote greater equity in health;

  • Carrying out developmental programs designed to promote education and foster learning;

  • Emergency relief operations for disaster victims and natural calamities for immediate help

Interaction with our Community

We provide an online forum as our platform to communicate. This is an open and mutual communication platform that allows employees to discuss our CSR initiatives, programs and activities freely.


All employees are allowed to post topics and hold discussions on the forum.
